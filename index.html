Act 2: Form complete sentences into the present progressive.  Do not forget you need the verb estar first.  Complete following before the activity.  

Conjugate the verb estar

yo -           estoy                                        nosotros - estamos

tú - estas

él/ella/Ud -         esta                                ellos/ellas/Uds. - estan

Place the endings for following present participles

Ar verbs : -ando

Er / Ir verbs: -iendo

Verbs that end in a vowel: -yendo
